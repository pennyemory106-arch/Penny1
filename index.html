// Proprietary Scaling

The Four Pillars of Commercial Book Scaling

We do not believe in generic marketing blasts or one-size-fits-all promo packages. Every campaign we manage is built upon our proprietary 4-Pillar Scaling Framework.

I.

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) & Packaging

Before initiating promotional outreach, your book’s sales page must be optimized to convert visitors into buyers.

  • Sales Copy Re-engineering: We craft hook-driven, emotionally resonant book descriptions formatted for readability.
  • Look-Inside Optimization: Structuring front matter to ensure readers are captivated within the first three pages.
  • Metadata & Category Mapping: Researching underground, high-traffic sub-genres to secure category rankings.
II.

Algorithmic Priming & Organic Discovery

Retailers operate on highly sophisticated search engines. We train them to push your book organically.

  • Keyword Indexing: Identifying the exact search phrases readers use when looking for your specific genre.
  • Also-Bought Engineering: Training retailer recommendation engines to associate your book with top-tier bestselling authors in your niche.
III.

Omnichannel Targeted Acquisition

We deploy targeted, scalable campaigns across the digital landscape to drive highly qualified reader traffic directly to your books.

  • Retailer Advertising: Hyper-targeted Sponsored Product campaigns targeting competitor book titles and author names.
  • Social Advertising (Meta Suite): Scroll-stopping visual hooks and campaigns targeted to verified avid readers within your genre.
IV.

Lifecycle Marketing & Reader Retention

Acquiring a reader is good; retaining them for your entire backlist is how author empires are built.

  • Series Sell-Through Optimization: Structuring back-matter to guarantee readers instantly buy book two after finishing book one.
  • Reader Magnet Integration: Building automated email sequences that turn casual readers into loyal superfans who buy every future release.
